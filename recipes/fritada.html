Fritada

Description

Fritada de chancho or Ecuadorian pork fritada is a popular weekend dish in Ecuador, in this traditional recipe the pork is cooked in a mix of water, orange juice with onion, garlic and cumin until the liquid is gone and the pork browns in the “mapahuira” or mix of its own grease and spices/flavors from the onion/garlic.

Ingredients

  • 3-4 lbs of pork
  • 1 tsp of cumin
  • 10 garlic gloves
  • 1 white onion

Steps

  1. Season the pork with the cumin, minced garlic, salt and pepper.
  2. In a large wok or a sauté pan add the meat, the chopped onion and shallot, the whole garlic cloves and the 3 cups of water.
  3. Cook on medium heat until the water is almost gone.
