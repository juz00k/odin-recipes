Bollo

Description

The Ecuadorian fish bun is a typical traditional dish from the coast. It is prepared by making a green banana dough, which is put into banana leaves, and filled with a fish conduit.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 pound of dolphin fish or yellow fin tuna.
  • 4 green plantains ground or processed
  • 1/2 pound peanut paste or peanut butter mixed with water
  • Salt. Pepper, Cumin

Steps

  1. Make a refrito with the annatto, tomato, green peppers, onions and garlic, spice it with salt, pepper, Ajinomoto, cumin and broth cube
  2. After some minutes put the fish cut in pieces and the peanut. Cook for 15 minutes.
  3. Ground or Process the plantains, cook them in a pot with water and salt.
